Harmonic Interface for EDO stuff

Explore Equal Divisions of the Octave (EDO). Use 12-EDO for standard Western tuning or experiment with higher subdivisions like 24-EDO for quarter tones.

You can use either your mouse and click on numbers to play each notes or using your computer keyboard (AZERTY or QWERTY layout) or use a midi controller !
I'm proud to anounce that it's responsive to midi velocity and that is GREAT to play with !
